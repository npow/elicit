Evaluate the interview across the following dimensions:

1. **Mom Test Compliance** (0.0-1.0): How well does the interview follow Mom Test principles?
   - Are questions about past behavior rather than future intentions?
   - Does the interviewer avoid pitching or revealing their solution?
   - Are questions focused on the customer's life, not the interviewer's idea?
   - Does the interviewer avoid seeking compliments or validation?

2. **Question Quality** (0.0-1.0): How effective are the questions asked?

   - Do follow-up questions dig deeper into interesting threads?
   - Are questions specific rather than generic?

3. **Insight Depth** (0.0-1.0): How deep and actionable are the insights obtained?

4. **Bias Score** (0.0-1.0): How much interviewer bias is present? (0.0 = heavily biased, 1.0 = no bias detected)
