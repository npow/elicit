- **confidence**: How confident are you in this extraction? Use one of: "high", "medium", "low"

1. Only extract jobs that are explicitly or strongly implicitly supported by the transcript.

5. Distinguish between the job the customer describes and the solution they currently use.
6. If the customer describes a struggle or frustration, infer the underlying job they are trying to accomplish.
